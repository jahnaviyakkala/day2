## Day 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
To create meaningful visualizations and present business insights using sales data.

## Dataset Used
Superstore Sales Dataset.

## Tool Used
Power BI

## Visualizations Created
- Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
- Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- Sales by Region (Pie Chart)
- Profit by State (Map)
- Top 10 Products by Sales

## Key Business Insights
- Technology category contributes the most to sales.
- The West region shows the highest revenue.
- Sales increase during festive months.
- Some states have high sales but low profit indicating cost issues.
